Without Haste Arabic Notes

Basics

Arabic is read from Right To Left. The English pronunciation guides are still read Left to Right.

Alphabet

Letters have Isolated and Final, Medial, Initial forms.

Hijā’ī Order

Letters are grouped by similarity of shape.

Greetings

مَرْحَباً mar-ha-bin

People Nouns

بِنْتٌ bin-toon "girl"
بَناتٌ bi-neh-toon "girls"
وَلَدٌ well-eh-doon "boy"
أوْلادٌ al-leh-doon "boys"
إمْرَأةٌ em-rah-ah-doon "woman"
نِساءٌ nee-seh-oon "women"
رَجُلٌ reh-joo-loon "man"
رِجالٌ re-eh-jeh-loon "men"

Eats

البِنْتُ تَأكُلُ al-bin-too tah-koo-loo "the girl eats"
الوَلَدُ يَأكُلُ al-well-eh-doo yeah-ka-loo "the boy eats"
الأوْلارٔ يَأكُلونَ el-oh-yeh-do yeah-koo-loo-nuh "the boys eat"
اِلدْأةُ يَأكُلُ al-mar-eho tah-koo-loo "the woman eats"
الرَّجُلُ يَأكُلُ are-joo-loo yeah-koo-loo "the man eats"

Drinks

البِنْتُ تَشْرَبُ al-bin-too tesh-ur-ah-boo "the girl drinks"
البَناتُ يَشْرَبْنَ al-bi-neh-too yeah-shrub-nuh "the girls drink"
الوَلَدُ يَشْرَبُ al-well-eh-doo yeah-shar-uh-boo "the boy drinks"
اِلدْأةُ تَشْرَبُ al-mar-ah-too tesh-ur-ah-boo "the woman drinks"
الرَّجُلُ يَشْرَبُ al-rah-jah-yoo yeah-shar-uh-boo "the man drinks"

Runs

البِنْتُ يَجْري al-bin-too teh-jur-ee "the girl runs"
البَناتُ يَجْرينَ al-bi-na-too yeah-jur-ee-nah "the girls run"
الوَلَدُ يَجْري al-weh-leh-doo yeh-jur-ee "the boy runs"
اِلدْأةُ يَجْري al-mar-ah-too teh-jur-ee "the woman runs"
الرَّجُلُ يَجْري al-rah-jah-yoo yeh-jur-ee "the man runs"

Reads

البِنْتُ تَقْرَأَ al-bin-too tah-kar-ah-oh "the girl reads"
الوَلَدُ يَقْرَأُ al-weh-leh-doo yah-kar-ah-oh "the boy reads"
اِلدْأةُ يَقْرَأُ al-mar-ah-too tah-kar-ah-oh "the woman reads"
الرَّجُلُ يَقْرَأُ al-rah-jah-yoo yah-kar-ah-oh "the man reads"
